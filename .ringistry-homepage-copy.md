## Section 10 — Materials & care (Elementor Tabs widget)
**H2:** Know what you're wearing

**Tab 1 — 925 Sterling Silver**
92.5% pure silver alloyed with copper for strength. Hallmarked and hypoallergenic. With basic care (a quick polish, kept dry when not worn), a sterling silver ring lasts a lifetime.

**Tab 2 — Aqeeq & Agate**
Aqeeq (also written akik) is a variety of natural agate. Yemeni aqeeq is the most prized — a deep, semi-translucent red believed in many cultures to bring calm and protection. Real aqeeq feels cool to the touch and shows natural color variation; we never dye our stones.

**Tab 3 — Sapphire & Moissanite**
Natural yellow sapphire (pukhraj) is one of the hardest gemstones on earth (9 on the Mohs scale) — only diamond is harder. Lab-grown moissanite (9.25) is harder still, has more brilliance than a diamond, and uses a fraction of the resources.

**Tab 4 — Care**
Remove rings before showering, sleeping, or working with chemicals. Polish with a soft cloth once a week. Store separately in a soft pouch. That's it.

## Section 11 — FAQ (Accordion + Rank Math FAQ schema)
**H2:** Questions people ask before buying

1. **Is your sterling silver real 925?** Yes. Every Ringistry ring is solid 925 sterling silver, the international standard for fine silver jewelry. It's the same alloy used by Tiffany, Cartier, and every major silversmith.

2. **What is aqeeq, and is your aqeeq real?** Aqeeq (also spelled akik or aqiq) is a natural variety of agate, traditionally prized across the Middle East and South Asia. Our aqeeq is real natural stone — we never dye, irradiate, or treat the color. Yemeni aqeeq, the rarest variety, has a distinctive deep-red translucence.

3. **What's the difference between Yemeni aqeeq and other agate?** Yemeni aqeeq is mined in specific regions of Yemen and has a deeper, more saturated red than agate from other sources. It's heavier per carat, slightly translucent in good light, and has been the prestige stone of choice in Islamic jewelry for over a thousand years.

4. **Do you ship worldwide?** Yes. We ship to every country with tracked international shipping. Free worldwide shipping on orders over $99.

5. **How long does shipping take?** Most rings ship within 3–5 business days. International delivery is typically 7–14 business days depending on country.

6. **Can I return a ring if it doesn't fit?** Yes. We accept returns within 30 days for any reason — sizing, change of mind, or just not feeling right. Send it back in its original condition and we'll refund or exchange.

7. **Are your rings hallmarked?** Yes. Each ring is stamped 925 on the inside of the band, certifying it as solid sterling silver.

8. **Is moissanite a real diamond alternative?** Moissanite is its own gemstone — silicon carbide. It's slightly harder than sapphire, has more fire and brilliance than diamond, and costs a fraction of the price. It's the most popular ethical alternative to mined diamond.
